# Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 29, 2026

`Infinite Wordle` is a five-letter word game. This privacy policy explains how the current version of the app handles information.

## Information The App Stores

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

- current game progress
- win and loss statistics
- streak information
- guess distribution
- theme preference

This information is used only to restore your progress and settings between sessions.

## Information The App Does Not Collect

The current version of `Infinite Wordle` does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal information such as:

- your name
- email address
- phone number
- location
- contacts
- photos
- microphone input
- payment information

The app also does not include ads, analytics, cloud sync, or social features in the current release.

## How Information Is Used

Any information stored by the app is used only for core gameplay features such as:

- restoring your current puzzle
- remembering your stats
- saving your visual theme preference

This data is not sold and is not intentionally shared with third parties by the current app release.

## Data Retention And Deletion

Locally stored data remains on your device until you remove it.

You can remove stored app data by:

- uninstalling the app
- clearing the app's storage from Android settings
- using the in-app stats reset option for gameplay statistics

## Children

`Infinite Wordle` is a general-audience game and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes To This Policy

If the app later adds analytics, advertising, accounts, cloud sync, or any new form of data collection, this privacy policy should be updated before that version is published.
